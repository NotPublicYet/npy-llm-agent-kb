# External Resources | 外部资源库

Purpose: capture time-sensitive news, official docs, knowledge articles, and high-value videos/links for LLM/Agent work.

目标：沉淀 LLM/Agent 相关的时效新闻、官方文档、知识文章与高价值视频/链接。

## Structure | 目录说明

- `news/`: important industry and ecosystem updates.
- `docs/`: official product/API/spec documentation links.
- `knowledge-articles/`: deep-dive articles, papers, and technical writeups.
- `videos/`: talks, demos, conference sessions, and walkthroughs.
- `link-digests/`: weekly/monthly curated link collections.
- `templates/`: note templates for consistent capture.

- `news/`：行业与生态更新。
- `docs/`：官方产品/API/规范文档链接。
- `knowledge-articles/`：深度文章、论文、技术解读。
- `videos/`：演讲、演示、会议分享、教学视频。
- `link-digests/`：周报/月报式链接精选。
- `templates/`：统一记录模板。

## Rules | 维护规则

- Prefer primary sources (official docs, repos, papers, standards).
- Every entry must include: date, source, summary, relevance, and confidence.
- News entries must include event date and publish date.
- Keep links stable; add archive snapshot when possible.
- Do not paste restricted paid content in full.

- 优先一手来源（官方文档、仓库、论文、标准）。
- 每条记录必须包含：日期、来源、摘要、相关性、置信度。
- 新闻必须同时写“事件日期”和“发布时间”。
- 链接尽量稳定，必要时附归档快照。
- 不得全文粘贴受版权/付费限制内容。

## Freshness SLA | 时效 SLA

- `news/`: triage within 48 hours.
- `docs/`: verify critical links monthly.
- `link-digests/`: publish at least once per week during active periods.

- `news/`：48 小时内完成分诊。
- `docs/`：关键链接每月校验一次。
- `link-digests/`：活跃期至少每周发布一次。
