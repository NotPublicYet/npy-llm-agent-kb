# Ideas Incubator | 创意孵化区

Use this area for early-stage concepts before they become formal questions or experiments.
用于承接尚未进入正式问题或实验阶段的早期想法。

- `seeds/`: raw ideas and prompts.
- `pitches/`: scoped proposals with expected value.
- `killed-ideas/`: intentionally stopped ideas with reasons.

- `seeds/`：原始想法与触发提示。
- `pitches/`：有范围和预期价值的提案。
- `killed-ideas/`：被明确终止的想法及原因。

Promotion rule: convert actionable ideas into questions under `01_questions/`.
升级规则：可执行想法应转化为 `01_questions/` 下的问题条目。
