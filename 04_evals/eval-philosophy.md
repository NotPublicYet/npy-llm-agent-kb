# Eval Philosophy | 评估哲学

- Evaluate to make decisions, not vanity metrics.
- Combine quantitative metrics and rubric-based qualitative scoring.
- Use stable benchmark sets for regression; synthetic sets for coverage expansion.
- Track tradeoffs: quality, latency, cost, safety.
- Require repeat runs before claiming improvement.

- 评估用于决策，不用于“好看指标”。
- 定量指标与定性 Rubric 结合使用。
- 稳定基准用于回归，合成集用于覆盖扩展。
- 显式记录质量、时延、成本、安全权衡。
- 宣称改进前必须进行重复运行验证。
